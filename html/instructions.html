Do not approach, feed, or harrass wildlife. Report data from hikes and cameras even when you do not see bear/signs. our data are solely for research. We keep it secure and confidential.

You can be a part of NYeBear in 3 ways:
  1. Go on hikes!
  2. Set a trail camera on your property!
  3. Coming soon: Monitor research sites!


If you see a bear in the wild:
  1. Stay calm.
  2. Do not run.
  3. Avoid eye contact with the bear.
  4. Facing the bear, back away slowly.
  5. If the bear approaches, make noise!
  6. Consider carrying bear spray and make sure you know how use it properly.



1. Go On Hikes (3 ways)

     *We recommend repeating hikes every few weeks to collect data for the same places*

▼ With the NYeBear App: Use this if you have a smartphone.
  1. Turn ON the "Start a Hike" function.
    • It is never too late to start a hike, even if you forgot to do this until you had an observation to report!
  2. When you observe a bear/ signs go to "Report Data" > "Report an Observation".
  3. Fill out the requested information:
    1. Only use "Use current location" if you are at the actual observation.
    2. Check all the signs that you observed.
    3. Rate how confident you are about the sign being from black bear
    4. Attach photo(s) of your bear observation or sign.
    5. Provide any other comments about your observation.
    6. Submit! Congratulations!
  4. When you finish your hike, turn OFF the "Start a Hike"/"Track Hiking" function.

▼ With print-out datasheets: If you don't have a smartphone, print out a paper version and take a GPS unit out with you.
  1. Print out a Hike Datasheet .
  2. Before you hit the trail/hike, fill out "Before the hike" and "Citizen Scientist Information" on the datasheet.
  3. When you start your hike, record the start time and GPS coordinates on the first row.
  4. At 10 minute intervals, fill out a row in the datasheet
  5. When you see bear/bear sign fill out a new row.
  6. When you are finished, provide any comments or thoughts about your hike or any bear signs or observations,
  7. Fill out "After the hike" at the top of the datasheet.
  8. Mail the form back to us, or submit it online by with the Quick Submit below:

▼ With the Quick Submit on this website: If you didnt have your smartphone or a hike datasheet.

  1. On the Quick Submit page, choose "Submit my hike data" .
  2. Fill out your email so that we can contact you in case you/we have questions.
  3. Then, provide the date and start time of your hike or observation.
  4. Fill out the remaining data.
  5. Using Google Maps, move the pin to your where you started the hike.Zoom in as close as you can.
  6. We recommend attaching photo(s) of your bear observation or sign.Include your hand, a coin or something in the photos for scale.
    • Upload a scanned copy of your datasheet.
  7. Provide any other comments about your observation.
  8. Submit! Congratulations!

2. Set A Trail Camera (2 ways)

     *We recommend submitting camera data frequently: once a week or biweekly**

▼ With the Quick Submit on this webpage: This is easiest.
  1. On the Quick Submit page, choose "Submit my camera data".
  2. Fill out your email so that we can contact you in case you/we have questions.
  3. Then, provide the dates and times of the camera period you are reporting.
  4. Fill out the remaining data about your camera.
  5. Using Google Maps, move the pin to your camera location. Zoom in as close as you can.
  6. Report which days the camera detected bears.
  7. Upload us your photos of the bears. Submit photos of other wildlife too!
  8. Provide any comments about the camera, bears or other wildlife.
  9. Submit! Congratulations

▼ With the NYeBear App: Use this if you will not be at a computer with your camera data
  1. On the home page, "Report Data" > "Report Camera Data".
  2. Fill out the requested information:
    1. Only "Use current location" if you are at your camera.
    2. Fill out the information about your camera./li>
  3. If your camera connects to you phone, attach your photos/videos.
  4. Provide any other comments about your camera check.
  5. Submit! Congratulations!
