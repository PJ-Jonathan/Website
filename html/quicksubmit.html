Thank you for being a NYeBear citizen scientist! Your data are valuable. To make sure we get the most out of your data, please review the Instructions. It is important to report data even when you do not observe bears or bear signs on your hikes or cameras. Knowing about the absence of bears is equally important as presence of bears for understanding bear distribution.
