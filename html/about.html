NYeBear is a citizen science project in New York where citizens submit information from hikes, trail cameras, and research sites about where they see (and donâ€™t see!) signs of black bears. Approximately 89% of land in New York is privately owned, so researchers need the help of citizens. This data goes towards helping researchers monitor the black bear population. The more people who participate and submit information, the more accurately researchers can determine the population size and range of black bears in New York.
