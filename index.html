Welcome to NY eBear!


Do you live in New York, or spend time outdoors hiking in New York? Ever wonder if there are bears where you live?

Become a NYeBear citizen scientist and join researchers at Cornell University studying the population size and range of black bears in upstate New York!

We are looking for citizen scientists who go on hikes with their smartphones or GPS units to record data about the presence and absence of bears and bear signs . Citizen scientists can also submit data from their personal trail cameras about the presence and absence of bears.

Explore the website to learn more and to submit your NYeBear data.

Please "bear" with us as we develop the NYeBear site. Check back soon!
